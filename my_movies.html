My Favorite Movies

Schindler's List

Storyline: In Poland during World War II, Oskar Schindler gradually becomes concerned for his Jewish workforce after witnessing their persecution by the Nazis.

Release Date: 4 February 1994

Director: Steven Spielberg

Stars: Liam Neeson, Ralph Fiennes, Ben Kingsley

Moneyball

Storyline: Oakland A's general manager Billy Beane's successful attempt to assemble a baseball team on a lean budget by employing computer-generated analysis to acquire new players.

Release Date: 23 September 2011

Director: Bennett Miller

Stars: Brad Pitt, Robin Wright, Jonah Hill

Avatar

Storyline: A paraplegic marine dispatched to the moon Pandora on a unique mission becomes torn between following his orders and protecting the world he feels is his home.

Release Date: 18 December 2009

Director: James Cameron

Stars: Sam Worthington, Zoe Saldana, Sigourney Weaver

The Dark Knight

Storyline: When the menace known as the Joker wreaks havoc and chaos on the people of Gotham, the caped crusader must come to terms with one of the greatest psychological tests of his ability to fight injustice.

Release Date: 18 July 2008

Director: Christopher Nolan

Stars: Christian Bale, Heath Ledger, Aaron Eckhart

Shutter Island

Storyline: A U.S Marshal investigates the disappearance of a murderess who escaped from a hospital for the criminally insane.

Release Date: 19 February 2010

Director: Martin Scorsese

Stars: Leonardo DiCaprio, Emily Mortimer, Mark Ruffalo

The Hurt Locker

Storyline: During the Iraq War, a Sergeant recently assigned to an army bomb squad is put at odds with his squad mates due to his maverick way of handling his work.

Release Date: 31 July 2009

Director: Kathryn Bigelow

Stars: Jeremy Renner, Anthony Mackie, Brian Geraghty

Iron Man

Storyline: After being held captive in an Afghan cave, an industrialist creates a unique weaponized suit of armor to fight evil.

Release Date: 2 May 2008

Director: Jon Favreau

Stars: Robert Downey Jr., Gwyneth Paltrow, Terrence Howard

Grown Ups

Storyline: After their high school basketball coach passes away, five good friends and former teammates reunite for a Fourth of July holiday weekend.

Release Date: 25 June 2010

Stars: Adam Sandler, Salma Hayek, Kevin James
